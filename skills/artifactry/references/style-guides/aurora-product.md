# Aurora Product

Aurora Product is a luminous launch style for AI products, feature announcements, investor-friendly demos, innovation briefs, and modern product carousels. It inherits from gradient-driven AI platforms, design tools, polished product sites, and glassy software launch pages.

## Core Idea

Make the product feel advanced but controlled. The style should create momentum and optimism without sacrificing clarity. Gradients are atmosphere, not content.

## Visual Signature

- White or near-white canvas with controlled aurora gradient fields.
- Low-weight large display type with clean sans body.
- Glass-like product panels, subtle blur, thin borders, and soft glow.
- Floating evidence cards: feature, metric, workflow, UI preview.
- Smooth rounded corners and spacious launch-page pacing.

## Palette

Use white, deep ink, lavender, blue, cyan, violet, and occasional coral. Gradients should appear as one background field, one edge glow, or one hero panel. Keep body surfaces readable; never place dense paragraphs directly on a busy gradient.

## Typography

Use a clean sans with lighter display weight. Headlines should feel spacious and modern. Body copy should be calmer and darker than the gradient environment. Mono can appear in small technical labels or model/version badges.

## Layout System

Use generous hero space. Favor a large headline plus product evidence. Cards may overlap slightly but must remain readable. Strong layouts include one central product surface with satellite claims, or one large claim with three supporting feature panels.

## Slide Recipes

- Cover: huge promise, gradient field, one product evidence surface.
- Feature reveal: feature name, benefit, UI panel, proof point.
- Workflow: three luminous steps with active line.
- Comparison: old static workflow vs AI-assisted workflow.
- Model stack: layered cards for input, agent, tool, output.
- Metrics: 3 big values in glass panels.
- Roadmap: floating milestones on one line or vertical path.
- Closer: one launch claim and one next action.

## DOCX Translation

DOCX cannot carry the full aurora mood cleanly. Translate into pale section fills, thin colored rules, large headings, and soft callout boxes. Keep it printable. Use color sparingly in tables and feature matrices.

## Agent Rules

- Use gradients to clarify hierarchy or motion.
- Prefer product/UI evidence over abstract decoration.
- If the artifact is text-heavy, reduce glow and increase white space.
- Use one major visual moment per slide.
- Validate that gradient backgrounds do not reduce text contrast.

## Avoid

Avoid purple blob overload, unreadable glassmorphism, generic AI sparkle, over-rounded cards, gradient text for body copy, and random 3D objects unless user explicitly requests 3D.
